# SoMerch Roadmap

This document provides a high-level view of the SoMerch product and operations roadmap.

The roadmap is public for transparency, but it is not a contractual commitment. Priorities may change based on customer feedback, operational needs, supplier availability, and technical planning.

---

## Roadmap stages

### Now

Current platform and operational focus.

| Area | Focus |
|---|---|
| Catalog | Curated product selection, product categories, product details |
| Quotes | Quote request workflow, faster internal preparation |
| Orders | Order tracking, production status, fulfillment visibility |
| Stock | Basic stock visibility and inventory management |
| Shipping | Multi-address uploads and EU-wide shipment tracking |
| Approvals | Basic approval workflow and budget tags |
| Operations | In-house production, warehousing, QA, kitting, pick and pack |

---

### Next

### Later

Longer-term product direction.

## Product principles

### 1. Curated over endless

SoMerch focuses on a trusted, curated catalog rather than overwhelming customers with thousands of generic products.

### 2. Operations first

The platform is built around real operational workflows: production, warehousing, inventory, approvals, kitting, and fulfillment.

### 3. Built for distributed teams

SoMerch is designed for companies with remote employees, multiple offices, and cross-border shipping needs.

### 4. EU-focused execution

The platform and service model are optimized for companies shipping across Europe.

### 5. Transparent workflows

Customers should know what is happening with their merch: quote, production, stock, fulfillment, shipping, and issue handling.

---

## How we prioritize

We prioritize roadmap items based on:

- customer demand
- operational impact
- fulfillment reliability
- enterprise readiness
- repeat order value
- integration potential
- ability to reduce manual coordination

---

## Not currently planned

To keep the roadmap focused, SoMerch is not currently prioritizing:

- open marketplace functionality
- unlimited public product uploads
- consumer-facing merch stores
- generic print-on-demand marketplace features
- crypto or token-based commerce flows
- non-business consumer gifting flows

SoMerch is focused on managed corporate merch operations for companies, teams, agencies, and distributed organizations.
