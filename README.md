# SoMerch Public Roadmap

**Merch that moves with your team.**

SoMerch is a software-powered corporate merch platform and service partner for distributed companies across Europe.

We help teams plan, produce, store, and ship branded merch from one place — combining curated products, in-house production, warehousing, inventory visibility, approvals, and EU-wide fulfillment.

This public roadmap gives customers, partners, and technical teams a transparent view of where the SoMerch platform is going.

## Why this roadmap exists

Corporate merch is no longer just about ordering branded items in bulk.

Distributed teams need better workflows for:

- onboarding packs
- employee gifting
- event kits
- office shipments
- multi-country fulfillment
- stock visibility
- budget control
- approval flows
- procurement reporting

SoMerch is building the operational layer that connects merch production, warehousing, fulfillment, and software into one managed system.

## Key product areas

### Catalog and product discovery

We are building a curated catalog focused on practical, durable, and brand-safe corporate merch.

The goal is not to offer thousands of generic items, but to make it easier for teams to choose products that work well in real company use cases.

Focus areas:

- curated best sellers
- product categories
- decoration methods
- product variants
- color and branding options
- sustainability attributes
- availability and lead-time signals

---

### Orders and approvals

SoMerch is designed for teams where multiple people may be involved in the merch process.

Focus areas:

- quote requests
- order status
- approval workflow
- internal notes
- budget tags
- department or project labels
- procurement-friendly records

---

### Inventory and warehousing

For teams that reorder often or ship to many locations, inventory visibility is critical.

Focus areas:

- live stock visibility
- SKU-level tracking
- batch tracking
- low-stock notifications
- reserved stock
- kitting workflows
- warehouse movements
- fulfillment status

---

### Fulfillment and shipping

Distributed companies need a reliable way to ship branded items to employees, offices, events, and partners.

Focus areas:

- multi-address uploads
- EU-wide shipping
- order splitting
- shipment tracking
- delivery status
- returned shipments
- issue handling
- fulfillment SLA visibility

---

### Employee and office portals

Future SoMerch workflows will make it easier for employees and office managers to claim, request, or receive merch without creating unnecessary work for operations teams.

Focus areas:

- redeem links
- office portals
- team stores
- group ordering
- shipment rules
- size selection
- address collection
- campaign-based fulfillment

---

### Integrations

SoMerch is planned to connect with tools used by HR, procurement, finance, and operations teams.

Potential integration areas:

- HRIS systems
- accounting exports
- procurement workflows
- SSO
- internal approval systems
- shipment tracking tools
- reporting dashboards
